Medinet Consults

Medinet Consults is a seamless medical video conferencing tool that is secure and easy to use.

Why choose Medinet Consults?

Reduces front office admin, allowing upload of images and medical documents. There is no more need to email, fax or post documents

Made specifically for health care professionals with privacy and security in mind – end-to-end encrypted and no data leaves Australia

Enable patient bookings for telehealth and face to face consults that integrate into practice management software

Simple and easy to use. No downloads, no patient logins. Works on phone, desktop or tablet

Split screen application - the practitioner can add clinical notes during the consult directly into the PMS

No more waiting for patients - be alerted when a patient has joined the consult room

How does it work?

Step 1

The health care professional enters a patient’s mobile number into Medinet Consult’s secure platform prior to the consultation.

Step 2

The patient receives an SMS with a unique link to the secure consultation and enters the virtual consult room.

Step 3

The health care professional is alerted when the patient enters the room and can begin the consultation.
