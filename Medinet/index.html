Our Doctors

All doctors consulting on Medinet are fully qualified and are registered with the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA), as required by the Medical Board of Australia, so you can be assured that you are receiving the highest level of health care.

Consult with GPs Online

Australian qualified doctors are available to consult with you 24/7 from the comfort of your home, or even from your desk.  If you require a follow-up appointment, simply book a face-to-face consultation with the doctor via Medinet.

All clinical advice and issuance of information takes place inside our app, keeping your medical information secure and private. 

Access Specialist Referrals

Our network of GPs can refer you to a relevant specialist doctor. You can also access upfront information about the specialists' fees, waitlist, location options and experience.

For the first time, you have all the information you need to make an informed decision about the right specialist doctor for you.

Have Your Medications Delivered

Our GPs can write you prescriptions for the medications you need. You can then choose to either pick up the medication at your preferred local pharmacy, or have it delivered to your door.

You can also buy other pharmaceutical goods such as supplements, wellness products and other over-the-counter medication. All your purchases are tracked and can be sent to you when you need it most.

All your Documents in One Place

We offer you a safe place to hold all your past and present medical certificates, specialist referrals and pathology results, so when you need them, you know exactly where to find them.

Our patient friendly platform is easy to read and share with any doctor you want to visit. We believe that your health data belongs to you, so we make sure that only you and the people who you want to can see it.

Pathology at Home

Sick of turning up to the pathology collection centre and then having to take a ticket and wait? We understand what it means to be sick and not want to get out of bed.

Soon, you will be able to request a professional pathology team member to visit you at home to have your tests conducted. You can also book a pathology appointment yourself at your most convenient collection centre now.

Find the Doctor Closest to you

Feeling sick on your way home? Via Medinet you can book in with your regular GP or find a GP closest to you. When you arrive at the medical centre, simply use your personalised QR code to conveniently check-in.
