Medinet Consults

Medinet Consults is a seamless medical video conferencing tool that is secure and easy to use.

Why choose Medinet Consults?

Made specifically for health care professionals with privacy and security in mind – end-to-end encrypted and no data leaves Australia

Simple and easy to use – no downloads or plugins required and it can be used on your phone, desktop or tablet

Fits easily into your current clinical workflows

No more waiting for patients - be alerted when a patient has joined the consult room

Ability to upload files in the video consultation

Australian doctor owned and led

How does it Work?

Step 1

The health care professional enters a patient’s mobile number into Medinet Consult’s secure platform prior to the consultation.

Step 2

The patient receives an SMS with a unique link to the secure virtual consultation.

Step 3

The health care professional is alerted when the patient enters the room and can begin the consultation.
