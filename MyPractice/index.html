Key features

Access prescriptions and certificates

Scripts generated from telehealth no longer need to be sent to pharmacies manually. We offer full automisation around processing of medical documents generated via telehealth consultations.

Manage referrals

Pathology, radiology and specialist referrals are all available for the patient straight after their consultation via the MyPractice app.

Appointment bookings

Patients can book and cancel appointments with their regular doctor via the MyPractice app.

Practice news and announcements

Send customised messages to your patients to create patient loyalty and help notify patients of upcoming changes or longer opening hours.
