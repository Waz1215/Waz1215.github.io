About

Medinet was created in 2019 by a group of Australian health care professionals who envisioned a universal platform that can help them connect to their vulnerable patients anytime and anywhere.

Whilst there will always be a role for face to face consultations, Medinet believes that online healthcare (telehealth) is an important adjunct in providing quality and accessible healthcare to all Australians.

With the widespread introduction of telehealth due to COVID19, there is an expectation that all healthcare practitioners can offer telehealth consultations. To help practitioners manage this more efficiently and securely, Medinet Consults was born.

Medinet Consults is a video/audio conferencing tool that can be used anytime, anywhere via any web-browser. Some of the biggest benefits of using this tool include simple registration with no need for the provider or patient to download any software and easy ability to upload pictures and files during consultation. Practitioners have applauded its simplicity and ease of use, which can be used by doctors, allied health care providers, hospitals and more.
